We want you to make our space safer. Help the industry grow and the ratters raise the war chest by doing what you do best: Shooting enemies!

Once you logged in, this website will track your PvP activity from zkillboard. Based on this data we give you BraveBucks for brave defenses, last hits and all the things that boost fast local responses. Once you reach a certain number of BraveBucks, we give you ISK. No bamboozles.

We believe encouraging fast local PvP responses can protect our space by protecting ratters and miners who again raise our ADMs. This again makes our space easier to defend with the current sovereignty system.

To get BraveBucks, your killmail must not be from a fleet fight (max. 20 attackers), not an awox and in one of the circled systems on the right. Empty pods are not counted. We may expand to Impass soon ;)

What determines how many BraveBucks you get per kill? Currently we pull the points from zkill, sqrt it and add 2 points for Final Blows. Soon we will add points for ADMs, the lower the more.
